Track overview

This track is part of the DriveX Grand Challenge 2026 within the broader DriveX initiative led by UCLA and developed with partner institutions and collaborators.

A challenge focused on cooperative perception using onboard vehicle sensor data and roadside infrastructure data in complex urban environments.

Detailed benchmarks, timelines, and organizer listings will be published here as they are finalized.
