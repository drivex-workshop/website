Initiative overview

What is DriveX?

DriveX is UCLA Mobility Lab’s public platform for physical AI in mobility, spanning embodied reasoning, world models, multimodal perception, cooperative intelligence, 3D/4D scene understanding, infrastructure-aware autonomy, and deployment-relevant evaluation.

Rather than isolated events, DriveX integrates workshops, challenge tracks, datasets, and seminars into a sustained multi-year program for the global research community.

Annual focus

2026–2027 flagship themes

You’ll see these ideas reflected in invited talks, challenge tracks, tutorials, and blog-style updates throughout the year.

Theme 01

Reasoning-centric autonomy

Advancing high-level scene understanding, embodied reasoning, and decision-making for autonomous systems operating in complex real-world environments.

Theme 02

World models and 3D/4D scene intelligence

Developing richer environment representations, world-grounded predictive models, and scene understanding pipelines for autonomy, simulation, and embodied AI.

Theme 03

Cooperative physical AI for real-world deployment

Bridging infrastructure intelligence, V2X, multimodal sensing, and deployment-oriented evaluation to support real-world intelligent transportation systems.
