What is DriveX?

A UCLA-led platform for physical AI in mobility.

DriveX is UCLA Mobility Lab’s public initiative connecting workshops, benchmark challenges, research assets, and seminars across autonomy, world models, cooperative intelligence, and deployment-ready mobility AI.
