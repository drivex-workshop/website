Programs

Program responsibilities

DriveX operates as a public research platform with recurring workshops and tutorials, benchmark-driven challenge tracks, open research assets, and community seminars.

  • Workshops & tutorials — recurring, cross-venue programs aligned with common annual themes.
  • Grand Challenge — benchmark tracks and evaluation programs connecting academia and industry.
  • Research assets — datasets, tools, and technical artifacts released for open reuse.
  • Seminars — invited talks and technical exchanges with researchers, students, and partners.

Edition-specific organizer lists are published on each event page as schedules are finalized.
