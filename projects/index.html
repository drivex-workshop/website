DriveX Projects

Focused research projects under the DriveX initiative, organized through UCLA Mobility Lab and partner institutions.

DriveX projects bring together research, infrastructure, and partner collaboration around specific themes that extend the broader DriveX program.

Overview

Project program

The DriveX project program is the long-form research track of the initiative. Each project organizes a sustained research effort around a specific scientific question, dataset effort, or deployment-facing system, while staying aligned with DriveX flagship themes.

Projects complement DriveX workshops, the Grand Challenge, research assets, and seminars by providing a longer time horizon and a smaller, more focused research community per topic.
